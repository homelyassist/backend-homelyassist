Why this Organisation?

The workers (Skilled / Semiskilled/ Unskilled) who are working in unorganised sector should know what the work opportunities for them at their locality are. It is not required for them to migrate for the same work and same compensation. Workers can stay at their locality and can lead a better life.

People living in small cities, towns and villages should get seamless, undisturbed service whenever they wish to by find the workers at their locality.

Workers should be paid the minimum wage set by Govt of India. It can ensure no exploitation like modern day slavery is happening.

The minimum requirements for a worker like minimum wage, timely payment, bank account, credit support, pension, health insurance, accident insurance should be provided.

Basic categories like help in areas (small cities, small towns and rural areas) in the category URBAN HELP, RURAL HELP, FOOD, HEALTH and CHILD EDUCATION are being supported.
